# Ingredients
meat of choice
1-2 heads of garlic
2/3-3/4 cup of soy sauce or Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup vinegar or apple cider vinegar
1-2 tablespoons of fish sauce
stock of choice(optional)

# Steps:
Brown garlic
sear meat
Fill with water just over the meat
Add soy sauce and vinegar
Bring to boil
Reduce heat to medium and cook about an hour or until desired tenderness is reached
